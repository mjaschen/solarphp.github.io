Go PHP 5!

Go PHP 5 is an initiative to encourage hosters and developers to leave PHP 4 behind and start using PHP 5 instead. We at Solar are happy to say that we have been PHP 5 E_STRICT from the very beginning. As of February 2007 we have required PHP 5.2 or later.

Read more about Solar and our goals, or go straight to getting started.
